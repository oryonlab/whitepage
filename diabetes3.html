Our resources were developed to provide information about lifestyle factors that research literature suggests may be relevant to general wellness for individuals with type 2 diabetes.

Scientific literature indicates that certain lifestyle considerations, when incorporated as a complement to medical treatment, may be associated with various wellness outcomes for individuals with type 2 diabetes.

Lifestyle Factors and Type 2 Diabetes

Type 2 diabetes is a complex metabolic condition. Medical treatment prescribed by healthcare professionals is the foundation of diabetes management. Research also explores various lifestyle considerations that may be relevant.

Our educational program provides information about these lifestyle elements, offering context for how they might be considered in conjunction with a healthcare provider's guidance.

By exploring this educational material, individuals may gain information that complements their physician-directed diabetes care plan.
